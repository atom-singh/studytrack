1. Product Name: StudyTrack
2. Who it's for: Indian students preparing for competitive exams (UPSC, JEE, NEET) who study 4+ hours daily across multiple subjects.
3. The problem: They don't know how their study time is actually split across subjects, and generic timers don't show subject-wise trends.
4. Solution — 3 core features: Timer per subject, Daily/Weekly charts, and Streak tracker.
5. How they'll find out: Instagram Reels showing study routines + Telegram/WhatsApp study groups.
6. Pricing: Free for up to 2 subjects, ₹99/month for unlimited subjects + weekly reports.
